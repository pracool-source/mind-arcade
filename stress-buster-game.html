ZEN SMASH
The Ultimate Stress Buster

Your stress isn't going anywhere on its own — so smash it. Literally. Pop every stress bubble before time runs out and watch your anxiety dissolve into thin air.

⚡ How To Play
1
Pop the Stress BubblesColoured bubbles drift across the screen — each one is a stress category. Tap or click to smash them and earn points.
2
Build Your ComboPop 5 bubbles in a row without missing = COMBO STREAK. Your score multiplier grows. Keep the chain going!
3
Catch the Green GemRare green bubbles appear briefly — they double your points AND lower your stress meter instantly. Don't miss them!
4
Watch Your Stress BarBubbles that escape (pop off-screen) raise your stress meter. If it fills up: GAME OVER. Stay sharp!
5
Levels Get HarderEvery 15 seconds the game speeds up. More bubbles, faster drift. How long can you last in Zen Zone?
6
Your Stress ReportAt the end, see which stress type you smashed the most — and get a personalised well-being message.
